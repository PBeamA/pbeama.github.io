I'm Beam, a final year PhD candidate in Finance at the University of Otago, Dunedin, New Zealand -- supervised by Professor Jin E. Zhang. I expect to submit my thesis for examination in May 2022 and join the Department of Accountancy & Finance as a Lecturer in Finance in June.

My research and teaching interest is primarily in the area of Derivatives and Quantitative Finance. I have also assisted in teaching BSNS114: Financial Decision Making, and FINC204: Personal Finance.

Before starting my PhD, I did a BSc, DipGrad, and MFinc at Otago.
